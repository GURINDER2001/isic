What is ISIC?

ISIC is an identity card which not only proves your student status but open the door for 20,000+ discounts at 1,50,000 outlets in more than 125 countries.

ISIC is endorsed by organizations such as UNESCO, the European Council on Culture and the Andean Community of Nations. The card is recognized by universities, academic institutions, student organizations, national governments and ministries of education around the world.
