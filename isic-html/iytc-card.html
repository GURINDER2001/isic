For youth looking to travel the world, the International Youth Travel Card (IYTC) is an internationally valid identity discount card which offers special rates on buses, accommodation and tours, allowing young travelers to travel more and spend less. Why Choose IYTC Card

The IYTC card is intended to provide a wide range of travel discounts to youth. There is only one condition to get this card and it is that you must be under 30 years of age on the date of issue of the card.

  • The card is an internationally accepted youth travel card- accepted both at home and across the globe
  • Travel Discount- Airline, bus, Rail, hotel booking
  • Other Discount – Accommodation, Food, Shopping, Sports, Historical Places, Amusement park and many more
  • Access to 1,50,000+ discounts in 125+ countries Endorsed by worldwide organizations such as UNESCO, UNWTO and the European Union Youth on the Move.
  • With 1 year or 365 days of coverage, it is ideal for those who love traveling.
