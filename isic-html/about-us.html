Company Overview

Stic Youth Travels Private Limited is the official Licensed Authority of the International Student Identity Cards (ISIC), International Youth Travel Card, for youth below 30 years of age (IYTC) and the International Teacher Identity Cards (ITIC) for teachers and academic employees – for the territory of the India, as a full member of the global ISIC association.

The company’s goal is to offer the Students, Teachers and Youth a wide range of special discounts and benefits in Education, Travel, Culture, Entertainment, Sports and others. We constantly strive to involve the ISIC Cards in the everyday life of Indian students.

All our team members try to make the ISIC / ITIC / IYTC cards a multifunctional tool for everyday life and use. A simple solution is to provide comfort, “one card for everything”.
