Referral Plan – FAQ

What is ISIC ‘Refer to Earn’ plan?

The ISIC ‘Refer to Earn’ plan lets you invite your friend to apply for an ISIC/IYTC/ITIC. You and your friend(s) both get rewarded for the purchase of the card. Your friend will get a discount of 10% (INR 50/-) on purchase of the card by using your ISIC/IYTC/ITIC serial number (14 digit alpha numeric serial number on right bottom of the card). You will receive INR 100/- in your Account / Paytm number after your friend’s successful purchase of ISIC with us. ISIC reserves the right to change or modify the referral program terms and conditions without informing the users. Also, ISIC can withdraw the program at any time without prior notice.

When and how will I and my friend get the referral benefits?

Your friend will get discount of 10% (INR 50/-) on purchase of ISIC card by using your ISIC serial number (16 digit embossed on right bottom). You will receive INR 100/- in your account within 30 days of your friend successful registration with us.

How many friends can I invite under ‘Refer to earn’ program?

You may refer unlimited number of friends for the card. ISIC will reward you for every successful purchase through your unique discount voucher code.

How can I refer ISIC to my friend?

Promote the cards to your friends and make them apply for ISIC/IYTC/ITIC online by using your ISIC serial number as a discount voucher code.

How will I get payment?

We will track all the successful registration from your unique discount code and transfer the money to your bank account by end of every month.
